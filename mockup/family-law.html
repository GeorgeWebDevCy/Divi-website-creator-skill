FAQ

Family Law Questions Answered

How long does a divorce take in Cyprus?+
An uncontested divorce typically takes 3–6 months. A contested divorce can take 12–24 months or longer. Our aim is always to reach fair resolution as efficiently as possible through negotiation where feasible.
Can foreigners get divorced in Cyprus?+
Yes. If at least one spouse has legal residency in Cyprus, a divorce can be filed in the Cyprus Family Court. We regularly handle divorces involving foreign nationals and cases with assets in multiple countries.
What are the grounds for divorce in Cyprus?+
Cyprus recognises irretrievable breakdown of the marriage as the primary ground, evidenced by separation of at least 4 years, adultery, abandonment, or behaviour making it unreasonable to continue the marriage.
How is child custody decided?+
Custody decisions are based on the best interests of the child. Courts consider the child's age, emotional bonds, each parent's ability to provide stability, and the child's wishes for older children. Shared custody is common.
How is the family home divided?+
If jointly owned, the family home is subject to a fair division order from the Family Court. This may involve one spouse buying out the other, or a court-ordered sale with proceeds divided.
